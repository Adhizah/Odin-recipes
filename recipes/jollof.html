Jollof

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved west african one pot dish made with rice,tomatoes,onion,variety of spices and a protein of your choice. It is a dish which is very popular in countries like Ghana,Nigeria and Senegal.The beauty of jollof rice lies in its versality ,that is,ingredients can vary from region to region,with each giving it a nice twist.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Habanero pepper
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Spices(ginger,garlic,curry powder,grounded rosemary)
  • Seasoning and salt to taste
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables(Green bell pepper,carrot,spring onions,lettuce and cabbage)
  • Ripe plaintain

Steps

  1. Wash your chicken thoroughly and place it in a pot over medium heat
  2. Blend together the ginger,garlic,onion and habanero pepper until smooth
  3. Pour the blended mixture over the chicken in the pot and add salt and your preferred seasonings to taste
  4. Cook it until tender, once cooked ,remove the chicken and fry it in hot oil until golden brown.Set aside
  5. Chop some onions and fresh tomatoes and then place another pot on medium heat and add a reasonable amount of oil.
  6. Stir fry the onion in the oil until soft and translucent,add the chopped fresh tomatoes and cook for few minutes
  7. Add the tomatoe paste to the mixture and continue cooking for sbout 10 minutes
  8. Once the mixture is well cooked,add the stock solution from the chicken and add yor preferred spices,seasoning and salt to taste
  9. Add alittle bit of water and stew and simmer for 5 minutes
  10. Add your washed rice to the mixture and stir to for a uniform mixture
  11. Add a small amount of water just enought to cook the rice but not too much to avoid making it too soft
  12. Lower the heat and cover the pot,allowing the rice to cook slowly.Stir occasionally to prevent burning
  13. While the jollof is cooking, peel your plantains and place it in a mixture of salt and water for few minutes
  14. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry the plantains until golden brown
  15. Wash your vegetables and chop them into desired shappes and sizes for serving
  16. Once the rice is fully cooked and liquid has been absorbed , your jollof is ready to be served. Serve the rice with fried plantains,chicken and vegetables.
