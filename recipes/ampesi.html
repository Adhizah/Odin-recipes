Ampesi and Kontommire Stew

Description

Ingredients

  • Palm oil
  • Onion
  • Habanero pepper
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Kontommire leaves
  • Eggs
  • Salted fish and salmon
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Yam and plantain
  • Salt and seasoning to taste
  • Pear
  • Garlic and ginger

Steps

  1. Wash the kontomire thoroughly and place it in a pot with a small amount of water. Boil the kontomire for 5-7 minutes until it softens.
  2. After boiling, remove the kontomire from the pot and grind or mash it into a smooth puree. Set aside.
  3. Wash the fresh tomatoes,ginger,garlic,onion and pepper. Blend the tomatoes, pepper, garlic, ginger, and a portion of the onion into a smooth paste. Set aside.
  4. In a separate pot, heat the palm oil over medium heat. Add the salted fish (koobi or smoked fish) and chunks of salmon to the hot oil and fry for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the chopped onion and stir-fry until soft and translucent. Add the blended mixture of tomatoes, pepper, garlic, ginger, and onion to the pot. Stir and cook the mixture for 10-12 minutes until it thickens.
  6. Add the tomato paste and continue stirring for another 5-7 minutes to enhance the flavor. Add the semi-mashed kontomire puree to the stew, stirring to combine the ingredients well.
  7. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until well-mixed, then pour the eggs into the stew. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for a few minutes before stirring gently to create soft lumps of eggs in the stew.
  8. Season with salt and seasoning cubes (if using). Stir and allow the stew to simmer for another 5 minutes. Set aside.
  9. In another pot, add water and a pinch of salt. Bring it to a boil. Add the peeled and cut yam and plantain pieces to the boiling water
  10. Wash the eggs and carefully place them in the same pot with the yam to boil together. Boil the yam and eggs for about 15-20 minutes, or until the yam is tender and the eggs are fully cooked.Once done, remove the eggs, peel off the shells, and slice them for garnish.
  11. Wash and peel the ripe pear (avocado). Slice it into thin, even slices to serve alongside the yam and stew.
  12. Serve the boiled yam alongside the kontomire stew. Garnish the plate with the sliced boiled eggs and pear (avocado) slices for a complete and delicious meal.
