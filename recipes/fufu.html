Fufu and beef light soup

Description

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Habanero pepper
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Garden eggs
  • Spices(ginger,garlic)
  • Seasoning and salt to taste
  • Okro
  • Cassava flour
  • Plantain flour

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Gradually pour the mixture of cassava and plantain flour into the boiling water while stirring continuously with a spatula to avoid lumps.
  2. Once the fufu is cooked and smooth, remove it from the pot and transfer it to an earthenware bowl
  3. Use your hands and a wooden masher (or spatula) to shape the fufu into round, semi-flat balls. Set the fufu aside, ready to be served with the soup.
  4. Wash the beef and place it in a pot. Blend the garlic, ginger, onion, and habanero peppers into a smooth paste.
  5. Pour the blended mixture over the beef, add salt and seasoning cubes (if using), and cook over medium heat for about 10-15 minutes until the beef is semi-cooked.
  6. In a separate pot, boil the garden eggs, tomatoes, and extra habanero peppers for about 5-10 minutes until soft. Once boiled, blend the garden eggs, tomatoes, and peppers into a smooth mixture.
  7. Pour the blended tomato mixture into the pot with the semi-cooked beef. Add the desired amount of water based on how thick or light you prefer your soup. Stir the mixture well and bring it to a boil.
  8. Wash the okro thoroughly and trim off the tips and heads. Add the whole okro to the soup, and allow it to boil along with the soup.
  9. Let the soup cook until the beef is tender, and the okro is fully cooked (about 15-20 minutes).
  10. Once the soup is ready, serve it hot with the prepared fufu. Enjoy your delicious meal!
